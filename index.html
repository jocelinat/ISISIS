Jocelin's Favorite Places in California

Los Angeles, San Diego, Catalina Island

Los Angeles

  • Los Angeles is where I am from and so I love the culture there.
  • It has been the home for the film industry of "Hollywood" for many generations.
  • At any time of the day you can find authentic and delicious food from all over the world.
  • There are so many beautiful beaches only a short drive away.
  • Famous landmarks like the Santa Monica Pier, Hollywood Boulevard, and the Griffith Observatory are fun to visit.

San Diego

  • San Diego is know for its beautiful sunsets and sunny skies.
  • Amusement parks like Sea World and Lego Land are great to go visit.
  • At La Jolla beach you can find awesome wildlife, like Sea Lions.
  • A famous landmark to visit is Seaport Village. There, you can find tasty food, a variety of markets, the Midway Ship, and a view of the ocean.
  • San Diego is less than an hour away from Mexico, great for a weekend getaway.

Catalina Island

  • Catalina Island is one of California's breathtaking Channel Islands.
  • At Catalina Island Marine Institute you can learn all about marine life at Catalina Island.
  • There are many fun activities to take part in like scuba diving, snorkeling, kayaking, swimming, hiking and so much more.
  • Catalina Island is actually part of Los Angeles County.
  • You can get to Catalina Island by ferry or helicopter.
